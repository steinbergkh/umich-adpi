Philanthropy

Alpha Delta Pi's national philanthropy is the Ronald McDonald House.

Throughout the year, we raise money and volunteer making dinners for the families staying at Ann Arbor's local Ronald McDonald House. We do so by hosting Mr. Greek Week Greek Week and throwing our annual fundraiser, ADPicnic in the spring.

The Ronald McDonald House is a place where families from all over the country can come and stay so that they can be near their children while they are being treated at the C.S. Mott Children's Hospital. The Ronald McDonald House has accommodated about 600 families per year since its opening in 1985. In 2011, another Ronald McDonald House was opened within the hospital which accommodates an additional 12 families per night.

To learn more about the Ronald McDonald House foundation visit their website at http://rmh-annarbor.org
