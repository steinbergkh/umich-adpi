Welcome to the Beta Eta Chapter of Alpha Delta Pi at the University of Michigan!

The women of Alpha Delta PI are a talented and diverse group of women who have been making their mark on Michigan's campus for over 80 years. Our time-honored traditions of striving for excellence and giving back to the community allow us to get involved, get a great education, and have fun during our years here at Michigan.

ADPi's open motto: "We Live for Each Other" truly represents the strong sisterhood encompassed by our sorority. At such a large university, it is important to find a smaller community of people who support you. "Going Greek" at U of M is a great way to make friends and get involved. I have made some of my best friends within the Greek system and the bonds that tie my sisters in ADPi and I together will last a lifetime. Whenever I meet a Beta Eta alum, there is an instant connection because of our membership in ADPi.

Our chapter is full of diverse and genuine women who have so much to offer by ways of support and knowledge. Alpha Delta Pi has given me life-long friendships as well as taught me lessons on how to be a better leader, a more dependable friend, a more organized student, and maintain philanthropic values. The experiences gained at Alpha Delta Pi cannot be replicated anywhere else on campus. I am proud to be a member of Alpha Delta Pi forever and always.
